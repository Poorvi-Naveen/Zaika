Special Selection

Delicious Menu

  • BENGALURU

    Bangalore, known for its vibrant food scene, offers a diverse range of culinary experiences that reflect its cosmopolitan nature. The city's food blends traditional South Indian flavors with influences from across India and the world.

  • UDUPI

    Udupi cuisine, rooted in the coastal region of Karnataka, is known for its fresh, vibrant flavors that reflect the area's natural abundance. The cuisine is light, aromatic, and delicately spiced, with a strong emphasis on coconut, tamarind, and locally sourced ingredients

  • COORG

    Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is a picturesque hill station in Karnataka known for its rich culture, coffee plantations, and unique cuisine. The food of Coorg is a blend of local ingredients, robust flavors, and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

  • HUBLI

    Hubli, located in northern Karnataka, has a cuisine that blends traditional North Karnataka flavors with regional influences. The food is known for its bold, spicy flavors and simple, hearty ingredients.
